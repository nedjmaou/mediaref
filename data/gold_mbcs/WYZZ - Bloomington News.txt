History
Founded in 1982, WYZZ – Bloomington News is a
[FOX](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/fox-news-bias/)-affiliated television station licensed to Bloomington, Illinois. The associated website is centralillinoisproud.com which provides news, sports, weather, politics, and crime reporting. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
WYZZ – Bloomington News is owned by
[Nexstar Media Group](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nexstar_Media_Group), which owns around 200 TV stations nationwide. The founder, chairman, president, and CEO of Nexstar is Perry A. Sook. [According](https://www.opensecrets.org/pacs/pacgot.php?cmte=C00567388&cycle=2018) to [Open Secrets](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/center-for-responsive-politics-open-secrets/), Nexstar Media group gave more money to Republicans in the 2018 election cycle (45% to Democrats, 55% to Republicans). However, in 2020 they gave more to Democrats (55% Democrats, 45% to Republicans). Advertising generates revenue.
Analysis / Bias
The WYZZ – Bloomington News website publishes news for the greater Bloomington, Illinois area. Local news features original reporting with minimal bias, such as this:
[Shooting sends man to the hospital Tuesday night](https://www.centralillinoisproud.com/news/local-news/shooting-sends-man-to-the-hospital-tuesday-night/). Many stories also contain videos from the TV Station. National and international news is covered through Nexstar Media Wire, the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/), and other large, credible media outlets.
Editorially, they do not produce op-eds; however, they report political news with minimal bias, such as this
[What’s in the House, Senate bills overhauling Jan. 6 count](https://www.centralillinoisproud.com/news/politics/ap-whats-in-the-house-senate-bills-overhauling-jan-6-count/). Reporting is reasonably balanced, covering both sides, and avoids using emotional language. In general, they report news factually and with minimal bias.
